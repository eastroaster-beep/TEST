FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

I've never traded before. Is Interactive Brokers right for me?
Absolutely. While IB is favored by professional traders for its advanced tools, we offer IBKR Lite — a simplified, commission-free experience built for beginners. You can also use our paper trading (simulation) account to practice with real market data before investing a single dollar. We also offer an extensive library of free courses, webinars, and educational videos.
Is my money safe at Interactive Brokers?
Yes. Interactive Brokers is a NYSE-listed, publicly traded company (IBKR) regulated by FINRA and a member of SIPC, which covers your account up to $500,000 (including $250,000 for cash). In addition to SIPC coverage, IB provides additional coverage through Lloyd's of London and other insurers. Your securities are held in a separate account, segregated from company funds.
What is IBKR Pro vs. IBKR Lite, and which should I choose?
IBKR Lite offers commission-free trades on US stocks and ETFs with no monthly fees — ideal for casual, long-term investors. IBKR Pro uses a tiered pricing model where active traders can pay as little as $0.0005 per share, and gains access to the full suite of professional tools, SmartRouting, and higher interest rates on idle cash. Both plans share the same account and can be toggled at any time.
How long does it take to open an account and make my first trade?
Most applications are approved within the same business day — often within a few hours. Once approved, you can deposit funds immediately via ACH transfer (US) or SEPA (EU). Funds are typically reflected in your account within 1–2 business days, at which point you can place your first trade. From start to first trade: often under 24 hours.
